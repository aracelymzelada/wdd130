Guatemala

Tikal, an ancient Maya city, Guatemala, sits deep, the rainforest, surrounded by towering trees and exotic wildlife. Its climate is hot and humid year-round. The site features massive pyramids, including Temple IV, offering breathtaking views over the jungle canopy. Heavy rainfall is common, especially June and September, making the ruins even more mystical
